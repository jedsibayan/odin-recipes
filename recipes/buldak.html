Buldak Recipe

Description

One of my favorite snacks to make, buldak noodles are for all my spice lovers out there!

Pair it with some meat, pork buns, or just itself alone, this spicy concoction will be sure to destroy and satisfy your stomach

Ingedients

  • Buldak ramen
  • Japanese mayonaise
  • White milk or heavy cream

Steps

  1. Heat water to a boil and place ramen inside
  2. Let noodles boil until firm
  3. Drain noodles and place back into pan
  4. Pour in milk and buldak sauce
  5. Let mixture simmer and reduce
  6. Top with cheese and Japanese mayo
  7. Enjoy!
