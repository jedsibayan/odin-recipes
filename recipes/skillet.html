Jed's World Famous Skillet Recipe

Descrption

A delicious and filling breakfast skillet. Makes for great leftovers

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Sausage and bacon
  • Eggs
  • Chopped onions

Steps

  1. Sautee onions then add potatoes shortly
  2. Let potatoes roast till they start to brown
  3. Add your meat. Pause
  4. Once everthing looks good to go add your eggs.
  5. Enjoy!
